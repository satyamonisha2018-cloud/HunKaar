Unrecovered amounts and loss months roll into the next month, so every target stays honest as new months are added.

Recovery movement follows cumulative net P/L after expenses, so loss months reduce recovered cost. Not recovered shows the gap against that car's monthly recovery target, including any unpaid recovery and current-month loss carried forward. Profit after recovery appears only when cumulative net P/L is higher than the full recovery target for that month.
